# I Ching (易经) Hexagram Meanings

## Overview
The I Ching, or Book of Changes, is an ancient Chinese divination text consisting of 64 hexagrams. Each hexagram is made of 6 lines (yin or yang) and represents a specific situation or archetype.

## Trigram Meanings

### Heaven (乾 Qian)
- **Symbol**: ☰
- **Element**: Metal
- **Meaning**: Creative force, strength, leadership, heaven
- **Keywords**: Action, initiative, power, success

### Lake (兑 Dui)
- **Symbol**: ☱
- **Element**: Metal
- **Meaning**: Joy, pleasure, communication
- **Keywords**: Happiness, expression, relaxation

### Fire (离 Li)
- **Symbol**: ☲
- **Element**: Fire
- **Meaning**: Clinging, brightness, clarity
- **Keywords**: Illumination, understanding, passion

### Thunder (震 Zhen)
- **Symbol**: ☳
- **Element**: Wood
- **Meaning**: Arousing, movement, shock
- **Keywords**: Action, surprise, awakening

### Wind (巽 Xun)
- **Symbol**: ☴
- **Element**: Wood
- **Meaning**: Gentle, penetrating, wind
- **Keywords**: Flexibility, influence, growth

### Water (坎 Kan)
- **Symbol**: ☵
- **Element**: Water
- **Meaning**: Abysmal, danger,陷
- **Keywords**: Challenge, depth, mystery

### Mountain (艮 Gen)
- **Symbol**: ☶
- **Element**: Earth
- **Meaning**: Keeping still, mountain
- **Keywords**: Rest, stability, contemplation

### Earth (坤 Kun)
- **Symbol**: ☷
- **Element**: Earth
- **Meaning**: Receptive, yielding, mother
- **Keywords**: Nurturing, patience, support

## Key Hexagrams

### 1. Heaven (乾 Qian)
**Lines**: ⚊⚊⚊⚊⚊⚊
**Meaning**: Creative force, pure yang energy
**Guidance**: Take initiative, be strong and persistent

### 2. Earth (坤 Kun)
**Lines**: ⚋⚋⚋⚋⚋⚋
**Meaning**: Receptive, pure yin energy
**Guidance**: Be patient, yield, nurture growth

### 3. Difficulty at Beginning (屯 Zhun)
**Lines**: ⚋⚋⚋⚊⚊⚋
**Meaning**: Initial obstacles, potential
**Guidance**: Persevere through early challenges

### 4. Youthful Folly (蒙 Meng)
**Lines**: ⚋⚊⚊⚋⚋⚋
**Meaning**: Innocence, learning
**Guidance**: Be open to learning, seek guidance

### 5. Waiting (需 Xu)
**Lines**: ⚋⚊⚊⚊⚋⚋
**Meaning**: Patience, preparation
**Guidance**: Wait for the right moment

### 6. Conflict (讼 Song)
**Lines**: ⚋⚊⚋⚊⚊⚋
**Meaning**: Dispute, argument
**Guidance**: Avoid conflict, seek mediation

### 7. The Army (师 Shi)
**Lines**: ⚋⚋⚋⚊⚋⚋
**Meaning**: Discipline, organization
**Guidance**: Work with others, maintain order

### 8. Holding Together (比 Bi)
**Lines**: ⚋⚊⚋⚋⚋⚋
**Meaning**: Union, alliance
**Guidance**: Seek cooperation, build relationships

## Changing Lines

When a line is "old" (value 6 or 9), it changes to its opposite:
- Old Yin (6) becomes Yang
- Old Yang (9) becomes Yin

The changing lines indicate:
- **Current situation**: The main hexagram
- **Future development**: The related hexagram (after changes)
- **Transition**: How to move from present to future

## Reading Technique

1. **Identify the main hexagram** - Current situation
2. **Note changing lines** - Areas of transformation
3. **Calculate related hexagram** - Future development
4. **Interpret the relationship** - Guidance for transition

## Example Reading

**Hexagram**: Heaven over Earth (11 - Peace)
**Changing lines**: 2nd and 5th lines
**Related hexagram**: Heaven over Heaven (1 - Creative)

**Interpretation**: 
Current harmony (Peace) is transitioning toward pure creative action (Creative). The changing lines suggest that balanced cooperation will evolve into strong leadership.

**Guidance**: 
Maintain current relationships while preparing to take initiative when the time is right.
