# Tarot Card Meanings

## Overview
The Tarot is a deck of 78 cards consisting of 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana. Each card has specific meanings that can be interpreted in readings.

## Major Arcana (22 Cards)

### 0 - The Fool
**Upright**: New beginnings, innocence, spontaneity, free spirit
**Reversed**: Recklessness, risk-taking, negligence
**Guidance**: Take a leap of faith, but be prepared

### I - The Magician
**Upright**: Manifestation, resourcefulness, power, inspired action
**Reversed**: Manipulation, untapped talents, deception
**Guidance**: Use your skills and resources wisely

### II - The High Priestess
**Upright**: Intuition, sacred knowledge, divine feminine, subconscious mind
**Reversed**: Secrets, disconnected from intuition, withdrawal
**Guidance**: Trust your inner voice

### III - The Empress
**Upright**: Femininity, beauty, nature, abundance, fertility
**Reversed**: Creative block, dependence on others
**Guidance**: Nurture yourself and others

### IV - The Emperor
**Upright**: Authority, structure, control, fatherhood
**Reversed**: Rigidity, over-control, lack of discipline
**Guidance**: Establish order and structure

### V - The Hierophant
**Upright**: Spiritual wisdom, religious beliefs, conformity, tradition
**Reversed**: Personal beliefs, freedom, challenging the status quo
**Guidance**: Respect tradition while staying true to yourself

### VI - The Lovers
**Upright**: Love, harmony, relationships, choices
**Reversed**: Disharmony, imbalance, misalignment of values
**Guidance**: Make choices aligned with your heart

### VII - The Chariot
**Upright**: Control, willpower, success, action, determination
**Reversed**: Self-discipline, opposition, lack of direction
**Guidance**: Take control of your direction

### VIII - Strength
**Upright**: Strength, courage, persuasion, influence, compassion
**Reversed**: Inner strength, self-doubt, raw emotion
**Guidance**: Face challenges with compassion and courage

### IX - The Hermit
**Upright**: Soul-searching, introspection, being alone, inner guidance
**Reversed**: Isolation, loneliness, withdrawal
**Guidance**: Take time for reflection

### X - Wheel of Fortune
**Upright**: Good luck, karma, life cycles, destiny, turning point
**Reversed**: Bad luck, resistance to change, breaking cycles
**Guidance**: Embrace change and life's cycles

### XI - Justice
**Upright**: Fairness, truth, law, cause and effect
**Reversed**: Dishonesty, lack of accountability, unfairness
**Guidance**: Seek truth and fairness

### XII - The Hanged Man
**Upright**: Pause, surrender, letting go, new perspectives
**Reversed**: Delays, resistance, stalling, indecision
**Guidance**: Sometimes you need to let go to move forward

### XIII - Death
**Upright**: Endings, transformation, transition, release
**Reversed**: Resistance to change, personal transformation, inner transformation
**Guidance**: Embrace transformation, endings bring new beginnings

### XIV - Temperance
**Upright**: Balance, moderation, patience, purpose
**Reversed**: Imbalance, excess, lack of long-term vision
**Guidance**: Find balance and practice patience

### XV - The Devil
**Upright**: Shadow self, attachment, addiction, restriction
**Reversed**: Releasing limiting beliefs, exploring dark thoughts, detachment
**Guidance**: Examine what holds you back

### XVI - The Tower
**Upright**: Sudden change, upheaval, chaos, revelation, awakening
**Reversed**: Personal transformation, fear of change, averting disaster
**Guidance**: Sudden changes can lead to better foundations

### XVII - The Star
**Upright**: Hope, faith, purpose, renewal, spirituality
**Reversed**: Lack of faith, despair, self-trust, disconnection
**Guidance**: Have faith in the universe and yourself

### XVIII - The Moon
**Upright**: Illusion, fear, anxiety, subconscious, intuition
**Reversed**: Release of fear, repressed emotion, inner confusion
**Guidance**: Trust your intuition, face your fears

### XIX - The Sun
**Upright**: Positivity, fun, warmth, success, vitality
**Reversed**: Inner child, feeling down, overly optimistic
**Guidance**: Embrace joy and positivity

### XX - Judgement
**Upright**: Reflection, reckoning, awakening, absolution
**Reversed**: Self-doubt, refusal of self-examination
**Guidance**: Evaluate your life and make changes

### XXI - The World
**Upright**: Completion, accomplishment, travel, wholeness
**Reversed**: Lack of closure, emptiness, incomplete
**Guidance**: Celebrate your achievements, embrace wholeness

## Reading Positions

### Three-Card Spread
1. **Past** - What has led to this situation
2. **Present** - Current circumstances
3. **Future** - Potential outcome

### Celtic Cross (10 Cards)
1. **Present** - Current situation
2. **Challenge** - What you're facing
3. **Past** - What led here
4. **Future** - What's ahead
5. **Conscious** - What you're aware of
6. **Unconscious** - Hidden factors
7. **Your approach** - How you're handling it
8. **External factors** - Outside influences
9. **Hopes/Fears** - What you desire/fear
10. **Outcome** - Final result

## Reversed Cards

Reversed cards don't always mean "negative." They can indicate:
- Internalized energy rather than expressed
- Blocked or delayed manifestation
- Alternative interpretation
- Shadow aspects of the card
